# 📚 English Vocabulary - 2026-03-08

## Perhaps

- **Definition:** Used to express uncertainty or possibility; maybe.
- **Pronunciation:** /pəˈhæps/
- **Part of Speech:** Adverb
- **Example Sentence:** Perhaps we should wait until tomorrow before making a final decision.
- **Synonyms:** Maybe, possibly, conceivably.
- **Notes:** Often used to make a suggestion or request more polite or less direct.

---
## Phonetic

- **Definition:** Relating to speech sounds or the representation of them in writing.
- **Pronunciation:** /fəˈnetɪk/
- **Part of Speech:** Adjective
- **Example Sentence:** The spelling of the word "cat" is almost entirely phonetic.
- **Synonyms:** Phonic, vocal, oral.
- **Notes:** Often used to describe a system of spelling where each letter represents a single sound.

---
## Phonics

- **Definition:** A method of teaching reading and spelling by correlating sounds with letters or groups of letters.
- **Pronunciation:** /ˈfɒnɪks/
- **Part of Speech:** Noun
- **Example Sentence:** Many primary schools use phonics as the foundation for their literacy programs.
- **Synonyms:** Phonetic method, acoustics.
- **Notes:** It focuses on the relationship between the symbols of an alphabet and the sounds they represent.
